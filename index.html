About Me

My name is Ben Lowry. I am a new developer eager to continue learning technologies and making programs and applications. I understand that getting into this industry and making yourself into a successful developer is about determination and continuous learning. Over time I am confident that I can demonstrate a deep understanding about a wide variety of technologies that will make me very appealing to a wide varitey of potential clientel and larger companies.

My first passion and what began as a hobby was learning the basics of programming. That passion has turned into a commitment as a student of the UT Austin coding bootcamp that I am currently enrolled in. I am using this program to further my career and gain the neccesary skills to become a full stack, versatile web developer. I enjoy working both with front-end and back-end technologies.
